Our Story

Founded in 2020, Paws & Whiskers was born out of love for animals and a desire to provide pet owners with trusted, high-quality products. We believe pets are family, and every wag, purr, and cuddle deserves the best care and comfort.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: To create a world where pets are happier, healthier, and stylishly pampered. We handpick every item with your pet’s needs in mind, ensuring safety, quality, and a little bit of fun.
